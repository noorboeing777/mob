About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is educating the students interest in Canada, giving them all the knowledge required to make a decision that will propel students career by:

  • assessing their needs, personality and interests, and providing them with the best options of where to study depending on their decision
  • supporting them through the admission process
  • handling their study permits into Canada
  • finding a proper housing that resonates with the student interests

Our Plan

We have two point of contacts, with the main office located in Greater Toronto Area and another office in Dubai This enables us to deal directly with all the stakeholders involved in the process, by being able to talk face-to-face with students and parents, and also being able to deal directly with universities, colleges, and everyone else involved.

Our Vision

  • We aim to position our services as to go option for students who wish to study in Canada.
  • We will provide support for everything needed throughout the process, empowering students with the knowledge and certainty of making the right decisions as to their study path, study permitting, settling and traveling into Canada.

Our Services

We intend to provide the assistance you need in every stage you need it

Student personality assessment

We will help you find those careers that resonate better for you based on your strengths, interests and objectives

Finding the best school

Whatever you want to study and where you want to study, we will provide you with those that are the best on the field you are pursuing

Admission preparation and application

Admission process can be challenging, but we are a team with Canadian education background,We will make sure that your application complies with everything the institution requires

Study permit

Our immigration-certified staff will make sure that your study visa application does not miss anything and will place you in a favourable position that will maximize your visa approval.

Flight booking and inland greeting

If you prefer not to spend time with tasks such as booking a trip and arranging inland arrival, we will take care of that for you. That way, you can spend more time planning on enjoying the country as soon as you arrive.

Housing

You are going to be living here for some years, so we want you to feel like home as soon as possible. Wherever you want to live at, there is an option for you in Canada. Let us do the searching and you do the enjoying.

Why choose Us

NVSC staff has year of experience in working with students through their path of studying in Canada. Our staff are certified by the ICCRC, which is the national regulatory body that oversees regulated Canadian immigration professionals. This means that you will be assisted by qualified and certified experts, and our staff also has experience on the Canadian education themselves. This will make your transition to Canada so smooth that you will enjoy this process instead of worrying about it.
